# Guild Clash

* Every M/W/F with prep day before


The guild has three status symbols:
* Rank
    * Determines amount of rewards we get for both victory and defeat
    * Higher rank == more rewards ($$$)
* League
    * Group of guilds we're matched against - changes at season end
* Ranking
    * Numerical position within the league, also gives rewards at season end

* We will be ranked based on points, which determines our league
* The goal is to defend strongholds - Everyone has their own
    * 3 Elite Strongholds (100 points if defeated)
    * 6 Standard Strongholds (50 points if defeated)
    * Basic Strongholds (10 points if defeated)
* After challenging an opponent, if you lose, they don't regain the health
    * If you can do at least 20% of a standard, or 10% of an elite - and there's enough people to finish the job - it's worth doing for more points.
* We get more points with successful defenses
* You get rewards for each challenge
* There's additional rewards based on stronghold level, and damage dealt
    * Capturing is bonus points

Key points:
* You can only attack any individual enemy stronghold once
* You can launch 3 attacks total
* Each stronghold can be captured up to 3 times
* Members will be ranked *within the guild* based on their contribution points
    * No rewards for ranking
    * More points == more rewards *for everyone*

## Guild Specifics
* Top 3 players on Elites
* Next 6 on Standard strongholds

* We will allow challenging players for their Standard stronghold spot. Ideally, meet them on the map, do a challenge, and have them use their defensive build, and defeat them. Then, use your build you want to use defensively, and have them lose to you. Post screenshots (or have their agreement) in our #clash channel, and a Deputy will swap the two of you.
* You **do not** get better rewards for having a better spot, but the whole guild gets better rewards for keeping the highest tiers safe!
    * The competition should be for fun, and to ensure the best defenders are in place. Spots will vary as strength fluctuates!
