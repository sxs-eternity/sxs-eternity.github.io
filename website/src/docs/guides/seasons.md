# Seasons
What is a Season?
Like many games, it's a period during which competition between players happens.
The top players are awarded vanity titles!
And absolutely nothing else of value, that isn't readily obtainable by any devoted F2P player!
If you score an SSS rank for the season, you will get the maximum Primostars, which you can use to activate Astral Pacts for permanent, rare stat bonuses!
The first is ATK +1%, and the second increases dungeon gear drop rate.

## Level Caps
Until now, we had timegates!
These are dates on which new content is unlocked for the entire server - Elite was the first, and Expert is the second.
Seasons have a level cap, however, you can continue to level after reaching it!
This additional level is your Season Level, and that's what's converted into Primostars at the end.
Season levels should turn green, indicating they will be reset to the last non-green level after the season ends.

## New Content
My understanding is new content will be released as new levels after a season reset, allowing us to play the content for a final time, before reaching new season content for the repeating seasons.
This will allow new servers to integrate existing players, and allow existing players to have *some* form of content during periods when the developers are still working on the next batch!

The example post in the official discord uses 100 as the dividing line between season and non season, with 100 being the last non-season level.

## Known Seasonal Items
* Level above 100,
* Fantomon level above 100,
* Gear enhancement above +100,
* Skill level above 100,
* Relic level above +10,

# Permanent Systems
The listed systems that are **not** reset after a season. "Essentially anything not mentioned above, but most importantly:"

* Skill ascensions
* Fantomon ascensions
* Fantomon aptitudes
* Guild buffs
* Companion affinity
* Relic ascensions
* Permanent dish buffs

## Season Gear
The Gem System is unlocked in the next season, which will give gear a huge stat boost!
There's also a ranking system it compete for who gets the best gear during the season!

Key Goals:
1. Reach SSS Rank for the season to unlock max benefits
2. Complete all non-season levels, so everything turns green, ensuring you've invested seasonal resources into permanent bonuses as much as possible
    * It may make sense to focus in one aspect first, going into seasonal content, like Wood/Stone in cart, so it's easier to level other stuff up later.
