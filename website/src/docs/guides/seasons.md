# Seasons
What is a Season?
Like many games, it's a period during which competition between players happens.
* The top players are awarded vanity titles!
* Gacha rolls and earnings are kept are kept
* If you score an SSS rank for the season, you should get the maximum Primostars, which you can use to activate Astral Pacts for permanent, rare stat bonuses!
    * The first is ATK +1%, and the second increases dungeon gear drop rate.
    * Tons of stat boosts from this, uncertain what cap is so far, but it gives you 10 + points/100 floored (round down)
    * You can check rank data at the telescope on your house!
* This is the chance to catch up! All players can reach SSS rank!
* Setup a good foundation, level your cart, and get ready for reset!

## Level Caps
Until now, we had timegates!
These are dates on which new content is unlocked for the entire server - Elite was the first, and Expert is the second.
Effectively, this acted as a level cap.
Seasons also have a level cap, however, you can continue to level after reaching it!
This additional level is your Season Progression Level, and that's what's converted into Primostars at the end.
Season levels should turn green, indicating they will be reset to the last non-green level after the season ends.

## New Content
You will play through the new content, then continue to level and complete harder dungeons, and explore a separate season map, until the season ends!
New content will be released as new levels after a season reset.
This will allow new servers to integrate existing players, and allow existing players to have *some* form of content during periods when the developers are still working on the next batch!

The example post in the official discord uses 100 as the dividing line between season and non season, with 100 being the last non-season level.

## Known Seasonal Items
* Level above 100
* Fantomon level above 100
* Gear enhancement above +100
* Skill level above 100
* Relic level above +10

# Permanent Systems
The listed systems that are **not** reset after a season. "Essentially anything not mentioned above, but most importantly:"

* Skill ascensions
* Fantomon ascensions
* Fantomon aptitudes
* Guild buffs
* Companion affinity
* Relic ascensions
* Permanent dish buffs

## Season Gear
The Gem System is unlocked in the first season, which will gives gear a solid stat boost!
There's also a ranking system it compete for who gets the best gear during the season!

1. Reach SSS Rank for the season to unlock max benefits


## Scores

There's several season scores in the telescope.
Keep in mind they're separate, with separate rewards, and make sure not to neglect any of them!

1. Overview - Basically the total of the other 3. Gives rewards at different points.
1. Gameplay - Easy to SSS, gives Gacha draws.
1. Progression - The total of your season levels, gives **Primostars**
1. Gear - Gives **Permanent % stat boosts** (I think they're permanent)
