# Tips
There's a lot of optimizations most people overlook!

## Why is this person stronger than me?
At the top, next to power, there is an icon a bit like `1|2` but with white/black panels.
This will compare your Combat Power to the other person's in a per category basis, so you can understand where they are getting more power than you.
Maybe there's optimizations there!
But maybe they just spent more.

### What does X stat do?
If you click the magnifying glass in your Character panel, or on anyone's profile, you can get a long list of each stat!
Tapping on those stats will give you a description of what they do, and even math on what they generate.

## EXP
AFK Speed ups can be saved until you complete all the map you can for the day.
Don't use them the second you login!

## Do Tests
Use the Test in skills to compare your DPS results, if you're doing DPS, or check how many rounds you survive against strong enemies otherwise.
