# General
Frequently Asked Questions

## Basics
With the exception of companions, most of the game systems are very forgiving.

* Slots are leveled, not the contents, so you can change your build easily
* Resonance - Unequipped things are automatically the same level as the lowest level in the lineup
* AFK Materials - As long as you check the game daily, you'll likely eventually pass people who spent more, who don't

## Catchup Mechanics
* Timegates - The first three major realms (Apprentice, Elite, Expert) unlock **server wide** at a certain time, usually at least 3-4 days after the first whale gets there. This gives F2P players a chance to catch up!
* Seasons - A sort of "Everyone resets back here" to the point new content ended. Aqualis marks the start of the first season, level 71+! I expect it to be one major realm (30 lvls) which puts it ending at lvl 100 - consistent with Moderator guides. Up to level 100, everything will be normal as it was. After, it will become a green season level!
    * Season levels allow you to gain more power and stats, and can be turned into Primostars later for permanent character rewards that last through resets!
    * Everyone can get max rating, the whales only get vanity titles, you can keep up even as F2P!
    * When new content is released, we will be reset to 100, do the new content, then have more season levels

## Resonance
### Gear
Every 5 levels gives resonance, requiring 5 pieces of gear at level +50 when the second class is unlocked.
* If you have something that hasn't reached resonance, don't waste resources leveling something else. Bring them all up to the minimum for that tier before upgrading your favorite, and then on.

### Relics
Relics that are displayed on the lineup get a 20% boost, but the rest still take effect! Resonance gives them the level of the lowest level in the lineup of that element.
* Make sure you get them to the same level before switching elements!
* Prioritize your class' relevant element!
* Then prioritize whichever element you have the best stats/most relics of!

### Fantomon
Fantomon are perhaps the most forgiving system in the game, almost impossible to mess up!
As long as you don't spend shards on something.

Fantomon have three unrelated states:
* Deployed
    * Floats/walks around and picks up items with you on the map!
* Battle Skills
    * Selected in your different skill builds!
    * You use the skill of this Fantomon in battle, regardless of if this pet is in the lineup!
* Lineup
    * Main gives a 100% boost from its stats! Pick something that makes sense for your build!
    * Sub pets give 50% boost from their stats! Pick one of the other two types, then another of your main's type!
    * Others - Still give 20% boost from their stats! Level them up with resonance! They match the level of the lowest in the lineup!

#### Key notes:
* Aptitude gives a ton of stats! Raise one of each type in your lineup!
    * You won't have enough items if you do two of the same type!
    * Aptitude can be reset *for free* in the top right of the panel! Don't be worried about switching your lineup later!
    * You're still leveling up slots, so don't be worried about switching the lineup!

## Companions
Companions, AFAIK, is not super forgiving.
You can't take back your gifts.
EOG has a [good guide here](https://www.eog.gg/games/sword-x-staff/guides/companion-upgrade/).

#### Key notes:
* Level everyone up to 20
* Bring class relevant companions to 50, as per the guide
* Try to use only preferred gifts
